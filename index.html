Hey Big Bear

I miss you i miss you so badly, its funny i have written multiple versions of this to just say that i miss you

I see the prints of your footsteps that marked all the small things of my life, from the meals i cook, to the lilies i water on my table, the ball under my bed, the music on my phone, any choral music i hear i find myself thinking damn she would have loved to hear that, the keychain dangling on my jeans, the notes you wrote puckered in my wallet gosh, i find myself carrying pieces of you in every place i visit. I miss kissing you, it felt like stars exploding every single time we kissed like you could take my very breath away and i would have let you take it all if i didn't get light headed so easily, i think my world stopped turning the day you had to leave, jay-e shoma khalist.

and now i am evermore certain i that no soul has never stood so close to my soul like you do and yet I stand here and realize i might never see your face or hear your voice again , and i begin to hate this life.

The reality of it all is that these feelings shall exist perhaps to the end of my days perhaps i shall miss you and desire for us to be for a lifetime but i think i should stop writing now i cannot burden you no longer with the state and existence of my feelings, i fear i can be too much to people sometimes i always want to bear it all.

i guess what I've been waffling about is that I hope things between us don't just end up being about timing or luck. I know last time we talked, you sounded pretty hopeless, like what's the point in hoping at all? but maybe it's okay to keep a little hope for better days

and just saying
amai hana iyi ndeyenyu parimwe zuva kana muchirikuda, mukafunga kuti muitore ahhh huyai muyitore muchimhanya.

and so ends my last letter to you

i am sorry for being so uncivil ❤️
